What learnings did you take from the chapter?

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Mindfulness helping me to decrease stress and maintain focus and just pay attention on what I should to and try not to get distracted. I found it very helpful to get released of anything around me and just trying to enjoy every moment of life even for a very short time.
