Some of the important things that I learnt from this chapter are: Understand what aversion and grasping are and that letting go of them changes the entire experience of pain and makes one suffer less. Not feeding the monsters of emotions. Understanding that emotions come and go and the duration of their existence depends on if we decide to feed them. How to deal with triggers using SBNRR

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I'm assuming we are needed to explain our experience of the Returning to Grounding exercise after the SBNRR exercise. I felt more confident and at the same time amused by my ability to control my emotions using this simple technique. After being aware of this, while performing the mindfulness exercise, as always there were thoughts and emotions arising at the back of my mind but I could focus on my breath much more easily and get less distracted.
