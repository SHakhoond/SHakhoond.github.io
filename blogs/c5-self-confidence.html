Self Confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They are similar concepts. Self awareness is being aware of one's state even when surrounded by a a mix of emotions. Mindfulness is keeping the focus/attention at the present moment accepting all the thoughts that could cause distraction. Attaining mindfulness can help develop self awareness.

Try atleast one of the self awareness practices from the chapter( body scan, journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I tried the body scan exercise. It made my entire body very relaxed and I almost felt as if I was going to fall asleep. Scanning through the different parts of the body I could sense stress in some of them but focusing on them individually relaxed them. When thinking about a happy event, I noticed movement in my eyes and lips as I was smiling and also the breathing changed a bit. Overall after the exercise, my body and mind were in a much more relaxed state and emotionally I was feeling more at peace and happier.

What did you take away from the “My emotions are not me” section?

I understood that my emotions are only temporary experience of how I feel at a particular moment and they do not define who I really am. And this means I can shift from one emotion to another if I want to. The sky and clouds analogy was a great help in understanding this.
